The problem

The gap
that kills.

Every mainstream emergency response system shares one fatal flaw: centralization. Panic buttons, GPS trackers, smartphones — all send information somewhere distant. Nobody sends it to the person standing three meters away or next door.

~11 min
Average ambulance response time across Europe — WHO target is under 8 minutes, provided a call was made
6 min
Time before brain damage becomes permanent without oxygen
∞ min
The gap the system was never designed to fill. If nobody calls — the ambulance never comes.
The solution

Community-first.
Decentralized.

Hive Synergy is the world's first decentralized, community-based emergency response layer. When a device detects an emergency, it instantly alerts the nearest people by physical proximity — no GPS, no central server, no GSM, no device pairing required. The neighbor is at the door before the ambulance leaves the station.

"Safety should not be a premium. It should be a baseline."

HiveShield
Monitor · Detect · Protect

Passive biometric monitoring that checks whether you're still alive. Automatic threat detection via HR spike, motion, strap removal, and covert gesture triggers.

HiveAlert
Broadcast · Locate · Respond

Offline BLE emergency broadcast. Device-to-device hopping. RSSI hot-cold guidance for responders. No SIM, no Wi-Fi, no GSM, no pairing required. Works where everything else fails.
